Facts about the Rainforest
  • Rainforests get 250 to 450 centimeters of rain every year.
  • Rainforests cover less than 3%, but they hold more than half the World's species.
  • About 30 million plants and animals live in the rainforest.
  • More than 2,000 species of butterfly reside in the rainforsets.
  • 80% of flowers found in the Rainforest are not found anywhere else.
