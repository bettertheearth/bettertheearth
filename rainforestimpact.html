How the Rainforest affects us
  • It will affect wildlife and ecosystems in a big way.
  • It will affect weather patterns.
  • It will affect the climate- climate change, that is- in a big way.
  • It will affect agriculture, farming, and the growing of crops.
  • It will cause desertification, or it will turn land into desert.
  • It will cause large amounts of extinction.
  • It will cause soil erosion, carving away at coasts and cliffs.
  • It will also affect the water supplies in certain areas.
