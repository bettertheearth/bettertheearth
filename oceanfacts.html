Some Fun Facts About the Beautiful Oceans! (that we are ruining..)
  1. Our Oceans cover more than 60% of the Earth's Surface.
  2. The majority of life on Earth is aquatic.
  3. Less than 5% of the Oceans have been explored.
  4. The largest mountain range is underwater.
  5. There are more historic artifacts underwater than in all the museums of the world.
  6. We only know about a fraction of the world's underwater speices.
  7. More than 70% of our oxygen is produced by the oceans.
  8. Lastly, the Pacific Ocean is the largest ocean, holding about 25,000 islands.
