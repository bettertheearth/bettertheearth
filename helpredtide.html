How you can help Red Tide
  • Pick up your pet's waste.
  • Stop using chemical fertilizers and pesticides.
  • Apply mulch and compost to build healthy living soil instead.
  • Plant native and climate-appropriate plants.
  • Direct rain gutters and downspouts into your landscaping to slow down and sponge up rain.
