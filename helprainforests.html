5 Things you can start doing NOW to help the Rainforests from deforestation!
  1. Support Indigienous Communities from the Rainforests.
  2. Create habitats for animals who are migrating to the rainforest. They help save the rainforest by keeping the food web in check.
  3. Buy responsibily sourced products.
  4. Buy enviornmentally responsible products.
  5. Stop buying palm oil. It's in many things from Doritos to Deoderant.
