Some Ways Ocean Pollution Effects Us
  1. 70% of the air we breathe comes from marine plant life that is destroyed by pollution in the oceans.
  2. 97% of the water we purify to drink comes from the oceans.
  3. Pollution essentially poisons fish that we could eat for food.
  4. If you come in contact with polluted water, it can cause rashes, stomache aches, and diaherria.
